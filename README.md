# 🐴 Outbound.AI: Simple End-to-End Guide to Finding Leads and Sending Emails

Hello, friend! If you feel confused by computers, tech terms, or "AI," do not worry at all! This guide is written in very simple, normal language. 

Think of this platform as a **digital post office**. Instead of writing and sending letters one by one, our platform helps you find people who might want to buy from you, writes the perfect friendly letter for you, and automatically mails it out!

Here is your simple, step-by-step guide to using this platform from start to finish.

---

## 🌾 The Overview: What does this platform do?
Instead of doing everything manually, this platform helps you:
1. **Find people to email** (Find Leads) from LinkedIn, Google Maps, or Instagram in 1 click.
2. **Setup your post boxes** (Domains & SMTP) so your emails look professional and don't get sent to the "Spam" folder.
3. **Write the messages** (AI Writer) using smart computer assistance to craft a great pitch.
4. **Send the emails automatically** (Outreach Campaigns) to your list of people.
5. **Read replies and write back** (Replies Box) all from one central dashboard!

---

## 🪜 Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Find Leads with AI (Get your lists)
This is the most exciting step! You tell the system who you are looking for, and it will gather their contact information.

* **What to do:**
  1. Click on **Outreach Campaigns** in the sidebar.
  2. Select your campaign from the list, or click **"Create Campaign"** to make a new one.
  3. Under the campaign, you will see a card called **🔍 Find New Leads with AI** on the right side.
  4. Type who you want to search for in the **Target Search Query** box (e.g., *"Dentists in Chicago"* or *"Coffee shops in Seattle"*).
  5. Select where you want to look under **Source Network** (e.g., *LinkedIn Only*, *Google Maps Only*, or *Instagram Only*).
  6. Choose exactly how many leads you want under **Number of Leads** (e.g., 10, 20, 30, or 50 leads).
  7. Click **"Generate Verified Leads"**!
* **What happens:** The system will search that specific platform, find the exact number of people you asked for, and instantly load them right into your campaign list. 

---

### Step 2: Set up Your Senders (SMTP & Domains)
To send emails, you need an email account configured.

* **What to do:**
  1. Go to the **SMTP Accounts Router** tab in the sidebar and click **"Connect SMTP Account"**.
  2. Fill in your email address and password.
  3. Enable **Warmup**: Our system will automatically send small friendly emails back and forth in the background to build your "reputation." This makes sure Google and Yahoo put your emails in the main inbox, not the junk folder!
  4. Go to the **DNS & Verified Domains** tab to view your domains. This proves you are a real business, which keeps you safe from getting blocked.

---

### Step 3: Write Your Message (Template Custom Writer)
Now we need to write the letter we want to send.

* **What to do:**
  1. Go to the **Template Custom Writer** tab in the sidebar.
  2. Tell the system what you do (e.g., *"We build beautiful websites for dentists"*).
  3. Select your tone (e.g., *Friendly*, *Professional*, or *Casual*).
  4. Click **"Create Template"**. The computer will instantly write a high-quality email template for you.
  5. Save this template to use inside your campaign!

---

### Step 4: Run the Campaign! (Outreach Campaigns)
Now, we match your message template with your list of found leads and hit go.

* **What to do:**
  1. Go back to **Outreach Campaigns** in the sidebar.
  2. Select your campaign and click the **Sequence** tab to review your subject line and message.
  3. Click the **Status** toggle at the top and set it to **"Running"** (or green).
* **What happens:** The system will start taking people from your leads list and mailing them your message automatically. It spaces them out nicely so it looks natural.

---

### Step 5: Read and Reply (Replies Intelligent Box)
When people reply to your emails, you will see them instantly!

* **What to do:**
  1. Click on **Replies Intelligent Box** in the sidebar.
  2. You will see a list of replies. Our system reads them and categorizes them:
     * 🟢 **Interested** (They want to learn more!)
     * 🔵 **Meeting Booked** (An appointment is scheduled!)
     * 🔴 **Not Interested** (They said no, thank you).
  3. Select a reply, and click **"Generate Custom AI Draft"**. The computer will read their message and write a reply for you (e.g., booking a time to talk).
  4. Review it and click **"Send Suggested Reply"** to email them back!

---

## 🌟 End-to-End Real Life Example

Here is exactly what it looks like in practice, step-by-step:

1. **You want to find clients for your software business.**
   * You click **Outreach Campaigns** in the sidebar and create a campaign called *"New York Gyms Website Offer"*.
   
2. **You want 20 leads from Google Maps only.**
   * Under your campaign, in the **🔍 Find New Leads with AI** box, you type: `"Fitness gyms in Brooklyn"`
   * You select **Source Network**: `"Google Maps Only"`
   * You select **Number of Leads**: `"20 Leads"`
   * You click **"Generate Verified Leads"**.
   * *Result:* The screen spins for a moment, and **exactly 20 Brooklyn gym contacts** appear on your screen with their names, gym names, and email addresses. They are added straight into your campaign!

3. **You draft your email message.**
   * You write a friendly email like:
     > *"Hi {{firstName}}, I saw your gym, {{company}}, on Google Maps! We build beautiful, high-speed websites that help gyms get more members. Are you free for a quick 5-minute chat next Tuesday?"*

4. **You launch the emails.**
   * You toggle the campaign switch to **"Running"**. 
   * Over the next few hours, the system automatically sends this email to all 20 gyms.

5. **The reply comes in!**
   * Two days later, John (owner of *Brooklyn Iron Gym*) replies: *"Hey! Our website is actually really old. What are your pricing options?"*
   
6. **You send the perfect answer.**
   * You open the **Replies Intelligent Box** and see John's email marked with a green 🟢 **Interested** badge.
   * You click **"Generate Custom AI Draft"**. 
   * The system drafts a response: *"Hi John, thanks for writing back! Our pricing starts at $499. Let's schedule a 5-minute call next Tuesday at 10 AM to discuss. Does that work for you?"*
   * You click **"Send"**, and John books a call!

You have successfully found a lead, contacted them, and started a business relationship with zero manual effort! 🚀
